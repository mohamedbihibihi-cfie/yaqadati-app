# Google Flow Prompts - Yaqadati

Les fichiers generes doivent etre ajoutes manuellement dans `public/assets/visuals` ou `public/assets/audio`.
L'application fonctionne aussi sans ces fichiers grace aux fallbacks CSS et Web Audio.

## Debut de seance - `start-session-breathing.mp4`

Prompt:

> Create a calm classroom projection background for primary school mindfulness. A soft luminous beginning, gentle warm morning light, slow breathing circle expanding and contracting, subtle halo, clean minimal composition, no text, no people, no logos, loopable 20 seconds, very slow motion, bright enough for classroom projection, peaceful but educational, not fantasy, not game-like.

Notes:

- Format conseille : MP4, boucle douce, 16:9.
- Mouvement lent : respiration visuelle claire, pas d'effets rapides.
- Ambiance : commencement, attention, installation.

## Apres la recreation - On ecoute - `recess-return-listen.mp4`

Prompt:

> Create a gentle classroom projection visual for pupils returning from recess and reconnecting with the teacher's voice. The image should suggest listening, attention returning to the class, and quiet presence, without showing people, text, logos, ears, or literal classroom objects. Soft blue-green light, slow settling motion, subtle waves moving toward a stable center, loopable 20 seconds, very slow motion, bright and readable on a classroom projector, educational and calm, not decorative overload.

Notes:

- Format conseille : MP4, boucle douce, 16:9.
- Fonction : annoncer le retour a l'ecoute.
- Ambiance : contact avec la classe, attention vers la voix de l'enseignant.

## Apres la recreation - On ralentit - `recess-return-slow-down.mp4`

Prompt:

> Create a gentle classroom projection visual for pupils returning from recess. A soft calming wave moves slowly from wider movement to stillness, cool fresh colors with a subtle green-blue tone, breathing rhythm visible through a slow expanding and relaxing circle, no text, no people, no logos, loopable 20 seconds, very slow motion, bright and readable on a classroom projector, calm educational atmosphere, not decorative overload.

Notes:

- Format conseille : MP4, boucle douce, 16:9.
- Ambiance : retour progressif au calme, ralentissement, concentration.

## Fond visuel general

Prompt:

> Create a very light abstract classroom mindfulness background for primary school learning support. Minimal clean space, soft teal and warm light accents, no text, no people, no symbols, projector friendly, calm but not sleepy, educational and focused, subtle depth, no dramatic gradients, no fast motion.

Usage possible :

- Inspiration pour des variantes futures.
- Ne pas integrer automatiquement dans la V1 sans validation.

## Ambiance sonore legere - `calm-background-loop.mp3`

Prompt:

> Generate a very soft loopable ambient sound bed for a primary classroom mindfulness activity. Gentle, non-intrusive, warm, almost silent, no voice, no melody that distracts, no percussion, no nature sounds that dominate, seamless loop around 30 seconds, suitable for teacher-controlled classroom projection.

Notes:

- Volume faible.
- Sans voix guidee.
- Boucle propre, sans rupture audible.

## Cloche de debut - `soft-start-chime.mp3`

Prompt:

> Generate a single soft classroom start chime, warm and gentle, less than 2 seconds, no reverb tail longer than needed, non-startling, suitable as a teacher-controlled signal for pupils to settle and prepare.

## Cloche de fin - `soft-end-bell.mp3`

Prompt:

> Generate a single soft end bell for a primary classroom mindfulness activity, gentle and clear, less than 2 seconds, calm closure feeling, no voice, no melody, non-startling, suitable for projected classroom use.
